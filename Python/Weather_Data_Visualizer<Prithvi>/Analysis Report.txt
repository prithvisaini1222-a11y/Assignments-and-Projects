# Weather Data Analysis Report

## 1. Introduction
I analyzed weather data containing Temperature, Rainfall, and Humidity.
The goal was to find trends and clean the data.

## 2. Dataset Info
- Rows: 100
- Columns: Date, Temp, Rain, Humidity
- Source: Synthetic (Generated for testing)

## 3. Key Findings
- The average temperature was around 30 degrees.
- Humidity and Temperature showed a loose relationship.
- Rainfall varied significantly month by month.

## 4. Conclusion
cleaned data and visualized. The hottest days did not always equal the most humid days.
